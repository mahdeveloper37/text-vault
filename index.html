How to Play

Interactive Fiction is played by typing commands in plain English.

  • Movement: Type NORTH, SOUTH, UP, or just N, S, U.
  • Interaction: TAKE LAMP, OPEN MAILBOX, READ BROCHURE.
  • System: SAVE to store progress, RESTORE to load, LOOK to see surroundings.
